Image Type Description Moisture Needs
Coleus Blumei Coleus Blumei, also known as Painted Nettle, is a tropical ornamental plant prized for its colorful and patterned foliage. It thrives in shaded areas and adds vibrant colors to gardens. 60%–80% moisture. Keep soil consistently moist but not waterlogged.
Green Pepper Green Pepper, or Bell Pepper, is a vegetable plant known for its sweet, mild flavor and is rich in vitamins A and C. It is widely used in cooking and salads. 50%–70% moisture. Water regularly to maintain even soil moisture, especially during fruit production.
Guldawari Guldawari, also known as Chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant popular for its large, vibrant blooms. It is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements. 40%–60% moisture. Prefers moist but well-drained soil. Avoid overwatering.
